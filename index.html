Yasin Farmani

Software Developer

Master of Human Computer Interaction

My name is Yasin Farmani . I obtained a Software Engineering degree from Shomal University and I am now a new student in Human-Computer Interaction at Carleton University. My research focus is user-centred design, user experience, , game and 3D interface , Virtual Reality user interface design. I have worked as a front end developer for more than four years at various private companies in my home country, Iran, and I also have experience in back-end development. I have considerable knowledge in coding in C# and PHP, and I am able to work with databases MySQL and SQL with entity framework. I have worked on more than 20 websites as a team member either directly or indirectly, and I was a team member on two large e-learning projects for elementary schools in my country.
